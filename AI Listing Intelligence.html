How it works

From a folder of raw photos to a market-ready listing.

Four steps. You stay in control of every photo and every word.

1

Upload photos + an address.

Drag in the shoot. Add the property address. That is the entire input — no forms, no manual data entry.

2

AI classifies and groups every room.

Each photo is auto-sorted by room and angle. Review the grouping, edit a label, or delete any photo you don't want in the report.

3

Get condition, market & neighborhood intel.

Per-room condition and quality on a 1–10 scale, comparable sales with a suggested range, neighborhood profile, and buyer-appeal positioning.

4

Order photos and publish a grounded listing.

Photos auto-order like a real walk-through. Refine the sequence, generate a market-grounded description in your writing style, and publish.
